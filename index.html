About us

We are Bookstore, the oldest bookstore in Egypt. Our Website might look unprofessional, but that's only because we don't have the money to hire a proper developer, instead we have to rely on college students who offer to do it for free just to gain some experience. It's not a secret that the world of reading is struggling right now, people are frying their attention span with TikTok and Instagram, no one reads books anymore we might as well just sell our books to fish. We are probably going out of business in a few years but we pray for nuclear catastrophe to come before we do, after all, death might be the greatest of all blessings. Anyway, if you want to buy any books, please do so before we drink this delicious cup of hemlock.
